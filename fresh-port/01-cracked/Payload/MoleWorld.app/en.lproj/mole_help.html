1. Basic Controls

  • Tap the screen to advance through story scenes and dialog.
  • Touch and drag to see more of the village.
  • Pinch to zoom in and out.
  • Touch the sign in the lower right-hand corner to access the build menu.
  • Touch the sign in the lower left-hand corner for the map screen you can use to see your friends' villages.
  • Touching a building or crop in progress will show the time remaining and how many Supershells are required finish it instantly. There's also a link to the Supershell shop to buy Supershells.
  • When viewing other screens, touching the button in the upper right-hand of the screen or touching anywhere outside the window will return you to your village.

The panel in the upper left-hand corner contains your current level, your Supershell total, your gold total, and a progress bar indicating how much XP is required before advancing to the next level. Touching your player icon will allow you to choose a new icon.

  1. Specialized Moles
    • Tap Uncle Bodhi for the current available quest objective.
    • Tap Fruits Shop, Bakery, Sylar's Studio, Mine, Lumber Mill (when there's an icon) to play a mini-game.
    • Tap Mole Man once a day for a mystery gift.

2. Options Menu

This menu can be accessed from the Main Menu.

  • Sound and Music

Toggles sound and music.

  • Notice

Turns push notifications on or off. Push notice tell you when your crops are grown, buildings completed, etc.

  • Restart Game

Wipes all save data. Do NOT press unless you want to clear all of your progress and start from the beginning. RESTARTING WILL PERMANENTLY REMOVE ALL OF YOUR SUPERSHELLS, AND ANY PURCHASED ITEMS.

3. Build Menu

The build menu is divided up into three tabs: Buildings, Decorations and Valuables. Just touch any of the tabs at the top to switch windows. Touch and drag left or right to highlight menu options. Some menu items may be transparent due to a lack of gold, lack of available Moles, or because it requires a higher level. Each one will specify the length of time it will take one Mole to build it. (Without the aid of Supershells) Each House or Cottage will add one Mole to your population, but can be upgraded after building Sylar's Studio (touch a house to see if it can be upgraded, and how much it will cost).

a.      Edit Mode

Touch the Edit button in the build menu to move objects in the village that have already been placed. Touch and hold objects to move them around, rotate them, or delete them entirely. This can include both objects and buildings you've added yourself, or objects such as bushes or flowers that might be in the way. Touching the house icon in the bottom-left of Edit Mode allows you to temporarily hide foreground objects, so that paths you've placed under buildings can be moved more easily. Touch the blue check mark to place an object. Touch the sign in the lower right again to exit Edit Mode.

4. Crops Menu

One garden can be added for each normal working Mole in your village (e.g. 8 Moles = max. 8 gardens). Touching an empty garden will bring up the crops menu, displaying all of the crops in the game. In the beginning some of these will not be available, as they require a higher level. Crops cost Gold to plant, and earn Gold and XP when they are harvested. As with buildings, a single crop will occupy a single Mole until it is fully grown. Tap a garden to plant a crop or to harvest one that is fully grown. Don't leave finished crops too long, or they could wilt. To cancel a crop that has already been planted, tap it once and then tap the red, circular icon.

5. Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How do I add more Moles?

You can add one Mole at a time by either building or upgrading another House or Cottage (upgrading requires Sylar's Studio.) Each house can be upgraded a total of two times, up to a maximum occupancy of 3 Moles. There will always be a limit to how many houses you can have in your village - but this limit will increase for each level.
Optionally, you can add an extra Mole instantly by purchasing one from the Valuables. Extra Mole will appear instantly, and do not require a house or cottage to live in or count towards the population limit.

  1. How do I add people to my friends list?

Please search for friends with MIMI number. Tap the panel the upper left-hand corner, MIMI number will show in the top of the screen.

  1. I can't launch the application, what should I do?

Try powering off your device, and then turning it back on again. Press and hold the button at the top of your device, until the "Power Off" slider appears on the screen, and slide it to the right. Wait for the device to shut down completely, and then press and hold the button again, to turn it back on.

  1. I paid for Supershells and I did not receive them. What should I do?

If you've lost Supershells, please contact service@61.com.tw. They'll be able to reimburse the missing Supershells.

  1. How do I prevent my game data from getting permanently lost?

There are a few things you could try:

    • Routinely sync your device with iTunes and keep your battery charged.
    • Occasionally reboot your device to clear the memory.
    • And (this is good advice in general) please keep all of your Supershell iTunes receipts.
